👋 Introduction

Welcome to GK Quiz – General Knowledge, developed and maintained as an independent app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Android application (Package: com.gkquiz.india.quiz_app).

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is designed to help you understand what data is collected, why it is collected, and how it is used.

By downloading and using GK Quiz, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

📋 Information We Collect

GK Quiz does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The following non-personal technical data may be collected automatically through third-party SDKs:

  • Advertising ID (GAID) – Used by Google AdMob to serve personalized or non-personalized ads
  • Device Information – Device type, OS version, app version (anonymous, for analytics)
  • Usage Analytics – Screens visited, session duration, in-app events (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash Reports – Anonymous error logs to improve app stability
  • IP Address – Collected by Google's ad network for geo-targeting ads (not stored by us)

⚙️ How We Use This Information

Information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To understand how users interact with the app and improve the experience
  • To diagnose and fix crashes or technical issues
  • To measure app performance and feature engagement

We never sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

📢 Advertising

GK Quiz is a free app supported by advertising. We use Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial ads. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to show personalized ads based on your interests and prior app usage.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Android 12+: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete Advertising ID
  • Older Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
Opting out of personalized ads does not remove ads from the app — you will still see non-personalized ads.

💾 Data Storage & Retention

GK Quiz stores only your quiz preferences and scores locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

We do not operate our own servers. No user data is stored on any backend server owned or controlled by us.

👶 Children's Privacy

GK Quiz is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

In compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and Google Play's Families Policy, our app is not directed at children under 13.

🔐 App Permissions

GK Quiz requests only the minimum permissions required:

  • INTERNET – Required to load advertisements (AdMob)
  • AD_ID – Required to identify device for ad targeting (Android 13+)
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – To detect connectivity for loading ads

GK Quiz does not request access to camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage, or any sensitive device permissions.

🛡️ Security

We value the security of your data and rely on industry-standard practices provided by Google's AdMob and Firebase SDKs, which use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

⚖️ Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Know – What data is collected and how it is used
  • Right to Opt-Out – Of personalized advertising (see Advertising section)
  • Right to Delete – Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
  • GDPR (EU Users) – You have the right to access, rectify, and erase personal data
  • CCPA (California Users) – You have the right to know and opt-out of data sale

We do not sell personal data, so CCPA opt-out rights regarding sale are not applicable.
